 Key Insights
- Chairs generated the highest revenue, followed by Printers and Laptops, while Phones generated the lowest revenue.
- The highest revenue was recorded in June, followed by May, while September recorded the lowest revenue.
- The number of customers was highest in June and lowest in September and November.
- Customers preferred Credit Card payments the most, followed by Online Payments, Cash, Gift Cards, and Debit Cards.
- The overall delivery rate is 19.25%, indicating that a relatively small proportion of orders were successfully delivered.

 Recommendations
- Improve the delivery process and increase the delivery success rate to enhance customer satisfaction and retention.
- Focus marketing and inventory efforts on Chairs and Printers, as they are the highest revenue-generating products.
- Reward the top three highest-revenue customers with discounts, loyalty benefits, or exclusive offers to encourage repeat purchases.
- Investigate the factors that contributed to the strong sales performance in June and implement similar strategies in other months to boost revenue throughout the year.
- Develop targeted promotions for low-performing months such as September and November to increase customer engagement and sales.
- Encourage the use of preferred payment methods by offering incentives and ensuring a seamless payment experience.
